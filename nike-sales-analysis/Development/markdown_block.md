Problem:
This project analyzes the stock price trends of Nike over time to understand how its market value has changed.

Success Definition:
The goal is to identify patterns, trends, and key statistics in Nike’s stock prices using historical data.

Methodology:
-Loaded the stock dataset into Zerve
-Cleaned and formatted the data
-Converted the Date column for time-series analysis
-Visualized closing prices over time using a line graph
-Calculated key statistics like maximum, minimum, and average price

Findings:
-Nike’s stock price shows fluctuations over time
-There are periods of both growth and decline
-The stock exhibits volatility, indicating changing market conditions
-Overall trend patterns can be observed through visualization

Conclusion:
This analysis helps in understanding historical stock behavior and highlights how data visualization can reveal meaningful financial insights.
